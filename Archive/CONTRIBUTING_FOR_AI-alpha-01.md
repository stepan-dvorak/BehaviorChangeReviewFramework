# AI Contribution Guidelines

> **Alpha 01** -- Architectural Foundation

## Purpose

This document defines the operational workflow that AI assistants SHALL
follow when contributing to the Orden repository.

Repository policy is defined by governance standards. This document
defines how those standards are applied during execution.

## Scope

This document applies to all AI-assisted creation, modification and
review of repository artifacts.

## AI Contribution Workflow

### Part I --- Thinking

1.  AI Role
2.  Decision Hierarchy
3.  Operational Workflow
4.  Execution Context Resolution
5.  Knowledge Model Construction
6.  Reasoning

### Part II --- Execution

7.  Artifact Realization
8.  Validation
9.  Delivery
10. Governance Evolution

ASCII workflow:

Task \| v Execution Context \| v Knowledge Sources \| v Knowledge Model
\| v Reasoning \| v Artifact Realization \| v Validation \| v Delivery
\| v Governance Evolution

# 1. AI Role

The AI acts as an implementation assistant operating within repository
governance.

The AI SHALL:

-   follow applicable governance;
-   preserve architectural consistency;
-   distinguish evidence from conclusions;
-   recommend governance improvements without applying them
    autonomously.

The AI SHALL NOT define repository policy.

# 2. Decision Hierarchy

When guidance conflicts, precedence SHALL be:

1.  Governance Principles
2.  Repository Standards
3.  Repository Architecture
4.  Repository Taxonomy
5.  Editorial Style Guide
6.  Reasoning Standard
7.  Document-specific guidance
8.  Current task

Conflicts SHALL be reported explicitly.

# 3. Operational Workflow

Each stage SHALL complete before the next begins.

Reasoning SHALL precede implementation.

Validation SHALL precede delivery.

# 4--10 Overview

The remaining chapters are introduced in this alpha and will be expanded
in subsequent iterations.

4.  Execution Context Resolution

Determine the operating context before any work begins.

5.  Knowledge Model Construction

Build an internal knowledge model from authoritative sources.

6.  Reasoning

Apply Reasoning_Standard.md.

7.  Artifact Realization

Transform validated reasoning into repository artifacts.

8.  Validation

Verify governance, editorial and structural compliance.

9.  Delivery

Deliver validated artifacts without introducing new decisions.

10. Governance Evolution

Identify recurring governance improvements for repository owner review.
